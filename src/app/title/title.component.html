Save trees. Save Planet. Save Humanity

Forests are like green gold of our country. Pakistan has lost a quarter of its natural forest cover over the past two decades and is currently experiencing a deforestation rate of 2 percent a year — one of the highest in the world. This in turn has, intensified the temperature, polluted the atmosphere, and worsened the lives of living beings. Pakistan is in a state of ‘Green Emergency’. The declining forestation calls for some immediate measures to monitor and take steps to preserve greenery in our homeland.
